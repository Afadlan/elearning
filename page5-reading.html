Reading Comprehension - Customer Service Call (B2)

Read the customer service phone conversation below and answer questions 6-10.

Customer Service Call - TechSupport Plus

Agent: Thank you for calling TechSupport Plus. This is Emma speaking. Before we begin, may I have your customer ID number for verification purposes?

Customer: Yes, it's TS-4789-2024. I'm calling because I've been experiencing some serious issues with my internet connection for the past three days.

Agent: I'm sorry to hear about the connectivity problems, Mr. Rodriguez. Let me pull up your account... I can see you're on our Premium Business package. Can you describe the specific issues you're encountering?

Customer: Well, the connection keeps dropping every few hours, and when it is working, the speed is significantly slower than what I'm paying for. I run a small online business, so this is really affecting my operations.

Agent: I completely understand how frustrating this must be for your business. Let me run some diagnostic tests on your line... I'm seeing some signal interference in your area. There appears to be maintenance work being conducted on the main fiber optic cables in your neighborhood.

Customer: How long is this maintenance expected to last? I wasn't notified about any scheduled work.

Agent: I apologize for the lack of communication. The maintenance was scheduled as an emergency repair due to storm damage last week. It should be completed by tomorrow evening. However, I'd like to offer you some compensation for the inconvenience.

Customer: What kind of compensation are we talking about?

Agent: I can provide you with a full credit for this week's service, which amounts to $47.50, and I'll also upgrade your connection speed at no additional cost for the next three months. Additionally, I'm setting up a priority support flag on your account.

Customer: That sounds reasonable. Will the upgrade be automatic, or do I need to do anything on my end?

Agent: The upgrade will be applied automatically to your account within 24 hours after the maintenance is completed. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details. Is there anything else I can help you with today?

Customer: No, that covers everything. Thank you for your help, Emma. I appreciate the quick resolution.

Agent: You're very welcome, Mr. Rodriguez. We value your business and apologize again for the inconvenience. Have a great day!

6. What is the customer's main complaint?

7. What caused the internet connectivity issues?

8. When is the maintenance work expected to be completed?

9. What compensation does the agent offer?

10. How long will the customer receive the upgraded connection speed?
