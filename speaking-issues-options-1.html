Traffic Congestion Problem

"Our city is facing a serious traffic congestion problem that affects thousands of commuters daily. Every morning and evening, the main roads become completely blocked with cars, buses, and trucks. People are spending two to three hours in traffic just to travel distances that should take thirty minutes. This situation is causing several issues: workers are arriving late to their jobs, students are missing classes, and emergency vehicles cannot reach their destinations quickly. The air pollution from idling vehicles is also becoming a health concern for residents. Additionally, local businesses are losing customers because people avoid shopping in areas with heavy traffic. The economic impact is significant, with millions of dollars lost in productivity each year. We need to find effective solutions to address this growing problem before it gets worse."

Read with appropriate concern and emphasis. Show the seriousness of the problem through your tone.

Instructions: Read the following dialogue about discussing options. Show the collaborative problem-solving process.

Discussing Solutions

Team Leader: "Now that we've identified the main issues with our customer service response time, let's discuss our options. What are some possible solutions we could implement?"

Team Member A: "One option would be to hire more customer service representatives. This would reduce the workload for each person and improve response times."

Team Member B: "That's a good idea, but it might be expensive. Another option could be to implement an automated system for common questions. This would free up our staff to handle more complex issues."

Team Leader: "Both suggestions have merit. We could also consider extending our service hours or creating a priority system for urgent requests. Let's evaluate each option based on cost, effectiveness, and implementation time. What do you think would be the most practical approach?"

Use different voices for each speaker. Show collaborative discussion and consideration of multiple options.
